About the UnitedPath Project

UnitedPath Community Hub was created entirely during the 2025–2026 school year by our TSA Webmaster team. All HTML, CSS, and JavaScript was written from scratch by team members — no website builder templates or pre-built themes were used.

All images used were sourced from publicly available platforms with attribution listed below. Framework systems were not used; the site was built with pure HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript.
