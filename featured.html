Design Brief Solution

Addressing the Annual Design Challenge

Our team researched how centralized digital resource hubs improve community access to essential services, particularly for underserved populations who face information barriers.

🔬

The Problem We Identified

Many community members — especially elderly residents and those with limited internet literacy — struggle to find and access local assistance programs, even when those programs exist and are available nearby.

💡

Our Research Approach

We interviewed local social workers, surveyed community members, and analyzed existing resource aggregation platforms to identify gaps and design best practices for digital community hubs.

🛠️

Our Solution

UnitedPath was designed with accessibility and simplicity at its core — searchable categories, expandable resource cards, a submission form for new entries, and a mobile-responsive layout for all devices.

📈

Impact & Results

The platform organizes 8+ resource categories covering food, health, education, employment, housing, seniors, family, and youth — providing a comprehensive, always-accessible community lifeline.
